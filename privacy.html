Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

Application

MaAM Dashboard - Invoice Read ("the Application") is a private, internal tool developed for Milli & Ali Medical LLC.

Data Collection

The Application reads invoice data from QuickBooks Online using the QuickBooks API. This data is displayed within a Google Apps Script web application accessible only to authorized users. No personal data is collected from end users beyond what is required for QuickBooks OAuth authentication.

Data Use

Invoice data is used solely for operational dashboard display (invoice status, revenue metrics, and collections tracking). Data is not sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

No QuickBooks data is stored permanently by the Application. Data is fetched on demand and displayed in the user's browser session. OAuth tokens are stored securely in Google Apps Script's built-in Script Properties.

Data Sharing

The Application does not share data with any third parties. Access is restricted to authorized personnel of Milli & Ali Medical and their designated technology consultant.

Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the application administrator.
